### Engagement Strategy

**1. Reply to these accounts** - their tweets get high visibility
**2. Add genuine value** - don't just agree, add a new perspective
**3. Be specific** - reference exact points from their tweet
**4. Ask questions** - start conversations
**5. Share results** - if you tried their advice, share outcomes

## What Works (From Research)
- Replies that add a **contrarian view** get more engagement
- **Questions** spark conversation
- **Personal experiments** with results perform well
- **Specific, actionable** advice beats generic tips

## Content Ideas to Post
1. "I tried [influencer's advice] for 30 days. Here's what happened:"
2. "[Specific tip] changed my workflow. Here's how:"
3. "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take on productivity]"
4. "Most productivity advice ignores [common problem]. Here's the fix:"

## Accounts to Avoid
- Politics, religion, controversial topics
- Anything requiring credentials (legal, medical, financial)
